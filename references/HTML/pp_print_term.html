pp_print_term : formatter -> term -> unit

SYNOPSIS
Prints a term (without quotes) to formatter.

DESCRIPTION
The call pp_print_term fmt tm prints the usual textual representation of the term tm to the formatter fmt. The string is just tm not `tm`. To print a term with colors, use pp_print_colored_term.

FAILURE CONDITIONS
Should never fail unless the formatter does.

COMMENTS
The usual case where the formatter is the standard output is print_term.
